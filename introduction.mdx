**octanist** is a service that was born out of the need for a better way to track offline conversions in online ad campaigns.

## Features

- **Easy to use dashboard**: The dashboard is easy to use and you can start tracking your offline conversions quickly.
- **API**: You can send offline conversions to the dashboard using our API.
- **Google Tag Manager template**: We provide a Google Tag Manager template that you can use to send leads to octanist.
- **Manage access for your team**: You can invite your team members to the dashboard and manage their access.

These are some of the features that make octanist a great tool for tracking offline conversions.
